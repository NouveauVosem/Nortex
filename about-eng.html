About Us

Nortex is a team of professionals who know everything about welding. We specialize in supplying modern and reliable welding machines and equipment directly from a trusted manufacturer in China. Our mission is to provide customers with high-quality equipment at an affordable price without compromising on reliability and functionality.

From the very beginning, Nortex has focused exclusively on inverter technology and has achieved significant success in building inverter welding machines. Before reaching the welder, the equipment goes through a full cycle of testing, which includes designing and testing various options for configuration, performance, and packaging. Even proper packaging requires a careful design approach. Therefore, whether we offer a machine for a beginner craftsman, a small workshop, or a large industrial enterprise where equipment operates 24/7, we provide a proven solution.

Our welding machines feature:

  • Support for multiple modes (MIG/MAG, MMA, TIG);
  • Digital control and intuitive interface;
  • IGBT technology and high energy efficiency;
  • Convenience and reliability for both professionals and beginners.

Why choose us:

  • Direct supply from the manufacturer without intermediaries;
  • Optimal price-to-quality ratio;
  • Warranty on all products;
  • Consultation and support at every stage — from choosing the machine to its operation.

We value our customers’ trust and strive to be your reliable partner in welding.
With Nortex, you get more than just equipment — you get solutions that work.
